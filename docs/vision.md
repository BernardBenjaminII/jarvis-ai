# JARVIS Vision

> **Knowledge is permanent. Intelligence is upgradable.**

---

## Vision

JARVIS is an offline-first, cross-platform AI operating environment.

Its purpose is to preserve, organize, retrieve, and amplify human knowledge while remaining independent of any single artificial intelligence technology.

Artificial intelligence will continue to evolve.

Knowledge should not have to.

JARVIS is designed so that models, embedding engines, vector databases, and AI frameworks can all be upgraded without requiring users to rebuild or migrate their accumulated knowledge.

Knowledge belongs to the user.

Intelligence is a service provided to that knowledge.

---

## Mission

Build an extensible platform that allows people to retain, organize, and interact with a lifetime of knowledge using the best intelligence available today—and tomorrow.

---

## Long-Term Goals

- Preserve knowledge across decades.
- Remain cross-platform.
- Operate offline whenever practical.
- Support modular AI engines.
- Enable specialized domain expertise.
- Minimize vendor lock-in.
- Encourage maintainable engineering.
- Make personal knowledge searchable and actionable.

---

## Engineering Philosophy

Every subsystem should support the following question:

> "Will this decision make the user's knowledge more durable, more accessible, or more useful?"

If the answer is no, the decision should be reconsidered.
